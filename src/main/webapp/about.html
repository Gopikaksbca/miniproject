About the Voting Portal

The Online Voting Portal is a secure and user-friendly platform designed to provide citizens with a simple way to cast their votes from anywhere. It supports both Domestic Voters and Overseas Voters, ensuring that every eligible voter has the opportunity to participate in the democratic process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make voting more accessible, transparent, and efficient by leveraging technology. We aim to increase voter participation and reduce barriers to voting, especially for those living abroad.
